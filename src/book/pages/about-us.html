V. Volta Volgate

Writer

Vee (they/them/theirs) is a writer and teacher who lives somewhere once before the floods. They’ve been tinkering around with the Worlds of the Lumerian System for many, many years. When their head isn’t in the clouds, Vee spends their free time swimming, trying out new recipes, and doting on their beloved cats.

Their writing career includes numerous community-related journalism pieces, poetry, and electronic literature. Hollow Reign began as the passion project they wrote in spiral-bound notebooks and hid under their pillow as a child, revived in various iterations over the years. It now exists here as a sort of shrine to all of the labor and love of the great becoming of what it means to toil in genre fiction as one hopes to become a writer and dreamer.
