Hollow Reign is an online web serial project, written by V. Volta Volgate. The primary intended audience is queer-identified young people, but all are welcome to read and enjoy. Hollow Reign has been through a great number of iterations since it came out of a dream the creator had in 2004, when they were just twelve years old. They’ve tinkered on in the Lumerian System off and on ever since. The story and format have changed a lot over the years, but the core is very much still the same.

This website is in many ways an archive of the most recent iteration of the tale. It is unfinished, representing one completed volume of what would be a six-volume project. The extended gaps between progress on the project and creative changes have yielded markedly different writing styles and narrative voices throughout. What exists here was primarily compounded on a Wordpress site in 2020, using much older writing and draft notes.

The creator hopes to return to this project one day.
